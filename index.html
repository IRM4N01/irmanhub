About Me

I come from a hospitality background, where I spent over seven years working in a fast-paced, customer-focused environment. While I valued the experience and skills I gained, I always felt the pull toward something more fulfilling — something that let me create, problem-solve, and build. I’ve always been fascinated by technology and software, curious about how things worked behind the screen and eager to learn how to build them myself.

After starting a family, I decided to take a leap of faith and pursue that long-standing passion. It hasn’t been a traditional path, but I’m now dedicated to learning software engineering and turning that curiosity into a career. With every project I build, I’m gaining not just technical skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, but also the confidence that I can create meaningful, lasting work in this field.
